Fabian Caba Heilbron

I am a PhD student at KAUST (Kingdom of Saudi Arabia) advised by Professor Bernard Ghanem. My research interests lie in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Specifically, I aim to tackle challenges of human activity recognition at large scale. I study efficient ways to harvest and annotate massive amount of data depicting human activities.

I received my Master degree in Electronics from Universidad del Norte in 2013, under the supervision of Professor Juan Carlos Niebles.
